Proprioception Workshops

An ongoing partnership with Cardiff Canine Citizens to create taster videos of their proprioception sessions

Proprioceptive - This system is responsible for many things in the body, it allows us and our dogs to be aware of where our body is in space without having to look at it. It is also important in many everyday movements, such aswalking up a step, but also the more complex movements, such as jumping over a jump.
